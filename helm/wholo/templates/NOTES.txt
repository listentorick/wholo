Wholo has been deployed to Kubernetes.

API:
  kubectl get svc {{ include "wholo.fullname" . }}-api

Portal:
  kubectl get svc {{ include "wholo.fullname" . }}-portal

For local development with hot-reload, port-forward the infrastructure:
  kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "wholo.fullname" . }}-postgresql 5432:5432
  kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "wholo.fullname" . }}-redis 6379:6379

Then run apps locally:
  pnpm dev:api
  pnpm dev:portal
